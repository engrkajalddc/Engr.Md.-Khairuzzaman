About Me

I am Engr. Md. Khairuzzaman, a professional Structural Engineer with extensive experience in planning, analysis, design, and supervision of reinforced concrete structures.

I completed my B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Bangladesh in 2008, and later obtained my Master of Structural Engineering and Construction from University Putra Malaysia (UPM) in 2020.

I have worked with both government and private consulting organizations, handling building projects of various heights using international codes such as BNBC, ACI, BS, and Eurocode.
